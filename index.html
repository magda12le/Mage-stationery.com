About Us

Mage stationery provides high-quality school and office supplies.

We sell notebooks, pens, pencils, files, markers, art materials, Counter books,

Rulers, tapes, Glue stick, rimu, rubber, Sharpener, correction pens and other more.

Also we have services such as Passport size, Photocopy, Typing, Scanning, Lamination,

Cards designing, editing and online application.

Our mission is to provide affordable and reliable stationery products.

Location
